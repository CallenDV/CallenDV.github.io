recipe: brownies

makes 40, probably.

ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons cocoa
  • 2/3 cups shortening
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups flour

instructions

  1. beat together the cocoa, shortening, sugar, eggs, baking powder, and salt. add flour slowly.
  2. spread on a greased cookie pan (with sides).
  3. at 350 fahrenheit, bake for 20 minutes or less, until the top has a dull crust and a slight finger imprint remains.
  4. put on frosting while brownies are hot.

extra stuff (frosting ingredients + instructions)

  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • 1/3 cup margarine
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • some salt i guess
  • enough milk for a spreading consistency

  • instructions: beat and spread on brownies
